About Us

Mission Statement

The Greenburgh Chamber of Commerce provides networking opportunities to Greenburgh businesses so that they may partner with the community, provide leadership and enhance a positive business environment.

Vision

The Chamber is a not-for-profit corporation for the benefit of the Greenburgh community and the enhancement of its business climate, prosperity and attractiveness. The purpose of the organization shall be to promote the economic, civic, and social welfare of its members, as well as the citizens of Greenburgh.

Mission Statements

The Greenburgh Chamber of Commerce gives local companies networking opportunities to help them get more involved in the community, show leadership, and improve the business climate.

Objective

The Chamber is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the business environment, profitability, and allure of the Greenburgh neighborhood. The organization's goal is to advance the social, civic, and economic well-being of both its members and the people of Greenburgh.
